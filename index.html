Welcome to CipherScribe! This tool can be used to encrypt a message. It's not a sophisticated tool, but it gets a job done.

  1. Type or copy&paste the message in the box below
  2. Click the ENCRYPT / DECRYPT button
  3. The encrypted message will appear in the blue box. Click the COPY button to save the text to your clipboard.
